📖 How to Use PromptLib

1

Explore AI Prompts

Browse through the extensive library of high-quality, pre-defined AI prompts designed for various tasks. Click on any card to view its full details.

2

Create Your Own

Log in and navigate to the 'Create' tab. Fill out the descriptive title, adjust the complexity slider, assign colorful tags, and paste your prompt to share it with the world!

3

Switch Themes

Use the Sun/Moon button on the top right next to 'How to Use' to switch between a bright, focused light mode and a sleek, modern dark mode.
